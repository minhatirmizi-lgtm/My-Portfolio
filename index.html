About Me

I am a BS Remote Sensing and GIS student with a strong academic foundation and practical experience in spatial data analysis, cartography, and WebGIS development. Throughout my coursework and independent projects, I have worked extensively with both raster and vector datasets to analyze real-world environmental and urban challenges.

My technical skill set includes working with GIS and remote sensing software such as ArcMap, ArcGIS Pro, ENVI, ERDAS, VS Code, GeoDa, and ArcGIS Online, along with hands-on experience in handling satellite imagery, spatial databases, and geospatial statistics. I have completed projects involving Urban Heat Island analysis, wildfire hazard mapping, population distribution, land use/land cover classification, and environmental risk assessment.
